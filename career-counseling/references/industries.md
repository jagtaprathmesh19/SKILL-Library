# Industry-Specific Career Paths Reference

## Technology

### Common Career Ladders

**Software Engineering:** Junior SWE → SWE → Senior SWE → Staff SWE → Principal SWE / Engineering Manager → Director of Engineering → VP Engineering → CTO

**Product Management:** Associate PM → PM → Senior PM → Group PM / Director of PM → VP Product → CPO

**Data:** Data Analyst → Senior Analyst → Analytics Manager → Data Scientist → Senior Data Scientist → Director of Data Science → VP / Chief Data Officer

**Design (UX/Product):** Junior Designer → Designer → Senior Designer → Lead Designer → Design Manager → Director of Design → VP Design / Head of Design

### Job Boards
- **General tech:** LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor
- **Startups:** Wellfound (AngelList), Greenhouse, Lever
- **Remote tech:** Remote.co, We Work Remotely, Remote OK
- **Engineering-specific:** Hired.com, Triplebyte, Otta
- **Senior / leadership:** Levels.fyi (comp), Riviera Partners, Karat

### Salary Ranges (US, 2024 approximations)
| Role | Entry | Mid | Senior | Lead/Staff |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Software Engineer | $90–120K | $130–160K | $160–200K+ | $200–300K+ |
| Product Manager | $80–110K | $120–150K | $150–190K | $190–250K+ |
| Data Scientist | $85–110K | $120–150K | $150–180K | $180–230K+ |
| UX Designer | $70–90K | $95–120K | $120–150K | $150–190K |

*Note: Total comp at public tech companies (FAANG etc.) often 2–3x base via equity.*

## Finance

### Career Ladders

**Investment Banking:** Analyst → Associate → VP → Director → Managing Director

**Asset Management / PE / VC:** Analyst → Associate → VP/Principal → Partner

**Corporate Finance (FP&A):** Financial Analyst → Senior Analyst → Finance Manager → Director → VP Finance → CFO

**Accounting / CPA track:** Staff Accountant → Senior Accountant → Manager → Senior Manager → Partner (public) or Controller / CFO (corporate)

### Key Certifications
- **CFA** (Chartered Financial Analyst): Gold standard for investment management; 3 levels; ~4 years
- **CPA** (Certified Public Accountant): Required for public accounting; varies by state
- **CFP** (Certified Financial Planner): For personal/wealth management
- **MBA**: Accelerates entry to IB, PE, strategy consulting; not always required

### Job Boards
- eFinancialCareers, Selby Jennings, Wall Street Oasis (community + jobs)
- For PE/VC: Headhunters are the primary channel (HSP, STO, Amity Search)

## Healthcare

### Career Paths (Non-Clinical)
**Healthcare Administration:** Coordinator → Manager → Director → VP → COO / CEO (hospital system)

**Health Tech:** Product, engineering, data roles follow tech ladders; domain knowledge is the moat

**Clinical Research:** CRC → CRA → Clinical Project Manager → Director of Clinical Ops → VP

### Clinical Tracks (overview for career changers)
- **Nursing (RN):** ADN or BSN → RN; specialize via certifications (ICU, ED, CRNA, NP)
- **Physician Assistant:** BA/BS → ~2-year PA program → PA-C
- **Nurse Practitioner:** BSN → MSN/DNP → NP (can prescribe independently in most states)
- **Public Health:** BA + MPH → health department, NGO, global health, policy

### Key Insight for Career Changers into Healthcare
The barrier to entry for admin/ops roles is lower than expected. Hospital systems and health tech companies value operational skills from other industries. Frame business acumen + mission alignment.

### Job Boards
- Healthcare-specific: Health eCareers, Health Jobs Nationwide, PracticeLink
- Health Tech: same as tech boards above + Rock Health community

## Consulting

### Career Ladders

**Management Consulting (McKinsey, BCG, Bain style):**
Analyst → Consultant → Engagement Manager → Principal → Partner → Senior Partner

**Big 4 (Deloitte, PwC, EY, KPMG):**
Analyst → Consultant → Senior Consultant → Manager → Senior Manager → Director → Partner

**Independent / Boutique Consulting:**
Domain-specific; seniority determined by client base and billing rate

### Breaking In
- **Pre-MBA:** Target analyst roles; case interview prep is essential (Case in Point, Victor Cheng, LOMS)
- **Post-MBA:** Associate-level; MBA from target school is the standard path
- **Experienced hire:** Leverage domain expertise; boutique consulting > MBB for non-MBA experienced hires
- **Independent:** Start with 1–2 anchor clients; referrals drive growth; build a niche reputation

### Case Interview Resources
- *Case in Point* by Marc Cosentino
- Victor Cheng's LOMS (Look Over My Shoulder) audio program
- Preplounge.com (practice with peers)
- Firm-specific prep: McKinsey has free practice cases on their website

## Marketing

### Career Paths

**Brand / Content:** Content Coordinator → Content Manager → Senior Content Manager → Head of Content → Director of Brand → VP Marketing → CMO

**Demand Gen / Growth:** Marketing Analyst → Demand Gen Manager → Sr. Manager → Director → VP Growth → CMO

**Product Marketing:** Associate PMM → PMM → Senior PMM → Lead PMM → Director of PMM → VP Product Marketing

**Agency Side:** Account Coordinator → Account Manager → Senior AM → Account Director → Group AD → VP / Partner

### Key Specializations in Demand
- **Performance Marketing / Paid Acquisition** (Google, Meta, TikTok ads): High demand, very measurable
- **SEO / Content-led growth**: Foundational for B2B SaaS
- **Marketing Analytics**: SQL + attribution modeling + GA4 / Amplitude

### Job Boards
- General: LinkedIn, Indeed
- Remote: We Work Remotely
- Community-driven: Exit Five (B2B), Demand Curve (growth), Superpath (content)

## Education

### Career Paths

**K–12:** Teacher → Department Head → Assistant Principal → Principal → Superintendent → District Leadership

**Higher Education:** Adjunct → Assistant Professor → Associate Professor → Full Professor → Department Chair → Dean → Provost

**EdTech / Learning & Development:** Instructional Designer → Sr. ID → Learning Experience Manager → Director of L&D → CLO

### Transitioning Out of Teaching
Teachers are frequently undervalued in career transition. Their skills are highly transferable:
- Instructional Design / L&D (most direct)
- Corporate Training
- Educational Sales (curriculum companies, EdTech)
- UX Research (user interviews, qualitative synthesis)
- HR / Talent Development
- Non-profit program management
- Curriculum writing / content development

**Key reframe:** "Classroom management for 30 teenagers" = stakeholder management, communication, crisis response, differentiated instruction, and data-driven adjustment.

## Entrepreneurship / Startups

### Stages and What They Need

| Stage | What They Hire For |
|---|---|
| Pre-Seed / Seed | Generalists who can do 5 jobs; people who've done the specific problem before |
| Series A | Functional leads (Head of Marketing, VP Sales, etc.) |
| Series B+ | Specialists, managers, process builders |
| Late Stage / Pre-IPO | Executives, scaled operations, formal structure |

### Joining a Startup: Risk Calibration Guide

Ask before joining any startup:
- What's the current runway? (How many months of cash)
- What's the path to next funding round or profitability?
- What's the cap table / equity structure?
- What's the last valuation and when was it set?
- What's the leadership team's track record?
- What does the board look like?

**Green flags:** Clear product-market fit signals, strong team retention, transparent leadership, growing revenue

**Red flags:** Runway under 6 months with no clear path, high leadership turnover, vague answers about business model, equity structure they won't share

## Non-Profit / Social Impact

### Career Paths
Program Coordinator → Program Manager → Director of Programs → VP Programs → COO / Executive Director

Development (Fundraising): Development Associate → Development Officer → Director of Development → VP Development → Chief Development Officer

### Breaking In
- The "passion for mission" is necessary but not sufficient. Show *skills*.
- Operational and business skills are highly valued and often scarce in nonprofits
- Volunteer leadership (board service, committee work) is a credible pathway

### Salary Reality Check
Nonprofit salaries are typically 10–30% below comparable for-profit roles. This varies greatly by:
- Organization size (large national nonprofits pay close to market)
- Geographic location
- Funding model

### Job Boards
- Idealist.org (largest nonprofit job board)
- LinkedIn NonProfit Hub
- Work for Good
- Chronicle of Philanthropy
